Chocolate Cake

A delicious and rich chocolate cake recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 3/4 cups flour
  • 3/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Grease and flour two 9-inch round baking pans.
  3. In a large bowl, stir together the sugar, flour, cocoa, baking powder, and baking soda.
  4. Add the eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla, and beat on medium speed for 2 minutes.
  5. Pour the batter evenly into the prepared pans.
  6. Bake for 30 to 35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
