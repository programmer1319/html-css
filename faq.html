Frequently Asked Questions

What is HTML5?

HTML5 is the latest version of the HyperText Markup Language, used to structure content on the web.

How do I learn HTML5?

Start with the basics by learning the different HTML5 elements and their purposes.

What are semantic elements?

Semantic elements are HTML5 elements that clearly describe their meaning in a human- and machine-readable way, such as ` <article>`, `<section>`, and `<header>`.
